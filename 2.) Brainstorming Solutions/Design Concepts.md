## General Concept

I envision a desk-clamp yoke inspired by the Thrustmaster Boeing yoke, using a hinged pivot that produces authentic pitch motion rather than the simple push–pull mechanism found in the Honeycomb yoke. By mounting the yoke on a clamp-attached frame with a dedicated pitch axis and realistic leverage, the design aims to replicate the tactile feel and control forces of a real aircraft yoke. This approach improves immersion, provides smoother and more precise feedback for fine inputs, and allows for adjustable preload and damping so the response can be tuned to match different aircraft types.
